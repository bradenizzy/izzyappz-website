Delete Your Account

Hands! AI Chef by IzzyAppz

You can delete your account and all its data at any time in the app.

How to request account deletion

  1. In the app: Open Hands! AI Chef, go to Settings (or Profile), and use the "Delete account" option to remove your account and all associated data.
  2. By email: If you prefer to submit a request manually, email support@izzyappz.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and the email address connected to your account. We will process your request and confirm once your account and data have been deleted.

What we delete

When you delete your account, we remove your account profile and all data associated with it, including recipes, cookbooks, preferences, and any other information stored for your account. We do not retain this data after deletion. Some data may remain in backups for a short period (typically up to 30 days) before being overwritten.
